SKEL@IIT, NCSR “Demokritos”

whoami

I am a Ph.D. candidate and a research associate at the Institute of Informatics and Telecommunications of the National Center for Scientific Research “Demokritos”.

I work along the CER Group and I conduct research mostly in the areas of machine learning and data mining under the guidance of Dr. Georgios Paliouras and Dr. Alexander Artikis. My research is currently focused on scalable semi-supervised learning of patterns for event recognition applications.

I hold a Diploma and an M.Sc. from the school of Electronics and Computer Engineering at the Technical University of Crete. During my studies I worked along the Kouretes RoboCup team where I completed my thesis “Dynamic Multi-Robot Coordination for the RoboCup Standard Platform League” under the supervision of Michail G. Lagoudakis. In 2014 I joined the CER Group where I completed my master thesis “Online Structure Learning for Markov Logic Networks using Background Knowledge Axiomatization”.

Apart from my research, I am fascinated by programming languages, software engineering, unix and free software.
